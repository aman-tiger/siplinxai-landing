**TL;DR:** Otter.ai pioneered AI meeting transcription, but its free tier is now limited to 300 minutes/month, it processes everything in the cloud, and its feature set has grown cluttered through years of pivots. Here are the best Otter.ai alternatives depending on what you actually need.

## Why Users Switch from Otter.ai

Otter.ai was one of the first consumer-facing speech-to-text tools to make real-time transcription accessible. That head start built a large user base—but also left the product carrying years of accumulated decisions that don't always serve modern use cases.

The most common reasons users switch:

**Free tier shrinkage.** Otter's free tier once offered substantially more. Today, 300 minutes per month and limited AI features make it impractical for anyone using it seriously for work. Once you exceed the free limits, pricing increases quickly.

**Cloud-only processing.** Every meeting you transcribe with Otter is sent to Otter's servers. For professional contexts—legal, medical, financial, or any situation covered by confidentiality agreements—this creates real compliance exposure.

**UX complexity.** Otter has added many features over the years: team workspaces, OtterPilot bot, AI chat, integrations. For users who just want clean notes from a meeting, the interface can feel cluttered and confusing.

**Accuracy gaps.** For technical vocabulary, non-native English speakers, or overlapping speech, Otter's accuracy can drop. Newer Whisper-based tools often perform better in these conditions.

### Fathom — Best for Zoom Users (Free)

Fathom's free tier has no meeting limits for individual Zoom users. If Otter's 300-minute cap is your primary frustration and you use Zoom, Fathom is the obvious replacement. It's cloud-based but clean, fast, and genuinely no-cost at the individual level.

### Fireflies.ai — Best for Team Workflows

If you need meeting notes shared across a CRM, project tracker, or team workspace, Fireflies has better integrations than Otter. The flip side: Fireflies' bot is visible in calls, which Otter's OtterPilot avoids on some platforms.

### Granola — Best Mac UX

Granola offers a polished native Mac experience with no bot joining your call. If you're Mac-only and want something that feels like a proper desktop app rather than a web wrapper, Granola is worth considering—with the same cloud-processing caveat as the others.
