## Teams' Built-In AI: What's Available and What It Costs

Microsoft has integrated AI capabilities into Teams through two layers:

**Teams transcription (free for Business/Enterprise):** Available in most Business plans, this produces a real-time transcript of Teams meetings. Transcripts are stored in OneDrive. Accuracy is reasonable but not exceptional.

**Teams Copilot (paid add-on):** Microsoft's Copilot for Microsoft 365 adds AI summaries, follow-up generation, and meeting recap to Teams. The cost: an additional $30/user/month on top of your existing Microsoft 365 subscription. For a 20-person team, that's $600/month just for AI meeting notes.

For many organizations, the cost-to-value ratio doesn't justify the Copilot add-on—especially when third-party tools offer comparable or better summaries at a fraction of the price.
